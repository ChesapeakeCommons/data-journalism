This map illustrates the extent of federally-regulated and reporting industrial facilities from EPA's Facility Registry Service database. Among these facilities, there are dozens of Toxic Release Inventory and Superfund sites - some of the most toxic sites in the watershed. However, there are hundreds more industrial sites throughout the watershed toxic materials that are subject to federal regulations for air, water, and hazardous waste pollution.
